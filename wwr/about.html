About Us

Guiding unforgettable river adventures since 2002.

Our Story

Dry Oar Whitewater Rafting started with three friends, one beat-up van, and a deep love for wild rivers. From a handful of weekend trips on the Snake River in 2002 to full seasons across the West today, our mission has stayed the same: create safe, inclusive, and joy-filled experiences on world-class whitewater.

Our guides are Swiftwater Rescue certified and trained in Leave No Trace. Whether it’s your first splash or your fiftieth, we tailor each trip to match your comfort level and stoke.

What We Value

  • Safety first—always
  • Stewardship of wild places
  • Beginner-friendly fun
  • Pro-level thrills
  • Memories that last
